### Reply 1

```text
Stuff mode:

1. Tag a charger, book, tool, or anything else
2. Set a small MON return bond
3. Your friend scans the QR and locks it
4. Confirm the return and their bond goes straight back
```

### Reply 2

```text
Money mode is a separate flow:

The lender funds an offer for one wallet. Only that borrower can accept it. Repayment returns the exact principal to the original lender and leaves an onchain receipt.
```
